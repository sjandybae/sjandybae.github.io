Q: What is Narrative Design?

As a narrative designer, I am most fascinated by game projects that have a storyworld and characters, and always strive to unify gameplay, art, and all other elements in its overall purpose and impact on players.

Games are capable of creating immersive, compelling, and meaningful stories experienced through play. In other words, game narrative design goes beyond merely written dialogue; it is about using the interactive features of a game to closely involve players in the world it creates.
