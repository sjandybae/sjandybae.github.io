About Andy Bae

Q: Who are you?

I have 5+ years of experience as a developer of video games, specializing in roles requiring narrative design and game design. From school, employment, and personal interests, I have done story writing, video editing, graphic design, programming, and many things in between.

It is through the accumulation of these various skills that I excel in collaborating with experts in their respective disciplines, and am confident in my ability to coordinate the team and lead projects to completion.

Q: What kind of games do you like?

I am most fascinated by narrative-driven game projects that have a storyworld and characters, especially when there is unity in gameplay, art, and all other elements in the game's overall purpose and impact on players.

Games are capable of creating immersive, compelling, and meaningful stories experienced through play. In other words, game narrative design goes beyond merely written dialogue; it is about using the interactive features of a game to closely involve players in the world it creates.

Education

In 2020, I graduated Simon Fraser University with a Bachelor of Arts (with Distinction) in Interactive Arts and Technology, in which I learned a wide variety of technical skills applicable to many industries.

I completed three co-op work terms where I worked for web and game-related companies, and also studied Storytelling Across Media in an international exchange in Sweden.

Interests

Aside from video games, my hobbies include writing short stories, reading comics, watching anime, and sometimes remembering to practice kendo or the drums. During university, I also co-founded a manga club and helped manage its events and projects for 5 years.
