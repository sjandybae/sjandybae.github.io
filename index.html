Andy's Game Projects

Yet Another Killing Game (YAKG)

#Ren'Py #Python #VisualNovel #MultipleEndings

A multilinear visual novel where the protagonist is trapped with two others, and depending on the player's choices, one of them will die for the rest to escape.

I created the story and project, wrote all dialogue and supporting Python code, and managed art production and publishing.

Pitcher and the Whale

#GameDesign #IndieDev #ProjectManagement #GameMakerStudio2

A 2D platforming game featuring a fox exploring a mountain and bringing water to a beached whale with his trusty bucket.

I led the game design and task management among a remote team, and handled publishing to Steam.
